About us

Founded in 2016, AITCO has rapidly established itself as a versatile and dynamic trading and contracting company dedicated to delivering excellence across multiple industries. We began our journey with a focus on trading high-quality women's clothing, abayas, bags, and shoes, expanding our portfolio to include comprehensive solutions in food supplies, gas stations, and service offices.

Our growth has been fueled by a commitment to quality and innovation, enabling us to meet the diverse needs of our clients with tailored solutions that uphold the highest standards.
